Exposure Defined

How does one get exposed?
Small pieces of information posted to social media frequently. Many small pieces of posted information can add up to large amounts of exposure.

Managing Exposure is Difficult

Social Media is now ubiquitous.
Posted information is effectively permanent.
Posted information has a global audience.
Privacy settings are inadequate to ensure protection.
A surprising and disturbing amount of personal information can be gleaned form the sum total of small pieces.

The Dangers of Exposure

Professional
70% of US Job recruiters have rejected candidates based on their online reputations.
10% of companies monitor social networking sites and blogs to track content about the company.
38% of college admissions officers found something online that had a negative impact on their evaluation of a student.
Community
Friends, colleagues, and officials can uncover embarrassing or compromising details.
Content submitted anonymously can be connected to you.
Information can be used to discredit you at PTA, HOA, and youth activities.
Criminal
43% of teenagers have been victims of cyber bullying.
1 in 10 cyber bullying victims tell a parent.
Bullying victims are between 2 and 9 times more likely to consider suicide
Online predators use many websites to find victims.
